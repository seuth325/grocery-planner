Steamed Fish with Ginger & Scallions

Delicate fish with aromatics and a light soy drizzle.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 fish fillets (cod)
  • 2 in ginger, matchsticks
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 tsp low‑sodium soy

Steps

  1. Steam fish with ginger until flaky; top with scallions.
  2. Drizzle a tiny bit of soy before serving.

Health Tips

Steaming keeps it lean; skip extra salt.
