Vegetable & Tofu Stir-fry

Colorful veg and tofu, quick and clean.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 14 oz firm tofu
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 1 tsp low‑sodium soy
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Sear tofu cubes; set aside. Stir‑fry veg; return tofu.
  2. Season lightly; splash of vinegar at the end.

Health Tips

Press tofu to crisp without much oil; keep sauce light.
