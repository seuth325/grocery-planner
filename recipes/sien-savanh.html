Sien Savanh (Lao Beef Jerky Salad)

Ingredients

  • 12 oz lean beef, thin strips
  • 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • Lime wedges, herbs, and sticky rice to serve

Steps

  1. Marinate beef with soy, fish sauce, honey, garlic powder, white pepper for 30+ minutes.
  2. Dry in low oven (200°F/95°C) or air fryer until chewy; quickly pan-fry to crisp edges.
  3. Serve with herbs, lime, and sticky rice.
