Sien Savanh (Lao Beef Jerky Salad)

Lean beef strips tossed with herbs, lime, and sticky rice.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced thin
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (low sodium)
  • Mint & cilantro handful
  • Sticky rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Dry in oven (lowest temp) or pan-sear strips until cooked.
  2. Toss warm beef with lime, fish sauce, soy, and herbs. Serve with sticky rice.

Health Tips

Lean cuts + short marination keeps sodium low and flavor bright.
