Tom Yum Soup (Chicken & Mushrooms)

  • 4 cups light broth, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaf, galangal (or ginger)
  • 6 oz chicken, mushrooms, cherry tomato (optional)
  • Finish with lime juice, fish sauce to taste
  1. Simmer aromatics 10 min; add chicken & mushrooms; finish with lime & fish sauce.
