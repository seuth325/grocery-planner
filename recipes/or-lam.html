Or Lam (Lao Herb & Eggplant Stew)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz mushrooms (shiitake or mixed)
  • 2 cups eggplant, chunks (Thai or Chinese)
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken or veggie stock
  • Handful dill
  • Kaffir lime leaves (2–3)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)

Steps

  1. Sauté onion and garlic; add mushrooms and eggplant, cook until softened.
  2. Add stock, lemongrass, kaffir leaves; simmer 10–15 minutes.
  3. Finish with fish sauce and dill. Remove lemongrass before serving.
