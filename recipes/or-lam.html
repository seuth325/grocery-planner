Or Lam (Lao Herb & Eggplant Stew)

Comforting stew with lemongrass, dill, mushrooms, and eggplant.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemongrass stalk, sliced
  • 1 small eggplant, cubed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken/veg broth
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Handful dill, chopped
  • 1 tsp toasted rice powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic and lemongrass; add eggplant and mushrooms, cook until softened.
  2. Add broth; simmer 10–12 min. Stir in spinach and dill at the end.
  3. Thicken with toasted rice powder if desired. Season to taste.

Health Tips

Use low‑sodium broth; the herbs provide big flavor without much salt.
