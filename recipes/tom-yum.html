Tom Yum Shrimp

Hot‑sour broth with lemongrass, kaffir lime, and shrimp.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 cups low‑sodium broth
  • 1 lemongrass stalk
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1–2 Thai chilies
  • 6–8 oz shrimp
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Simmer broth with lemongrass, lime leaves, chilies. Add mushrooms.
  2. Add shrimp until pink. Finish with lime juice.

Health Tips

Skip added salt; aromatics bring bold flavor.
