Ping Pa (Lao Grilled Fish)

Ingredients

  • 2 small whole fish (or 2 fillets cod/tilapia)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised & chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp neutral oil

Steps

  1. Mix lemongrass, garlic, ginger, fish sauce, lime juice, oil into a paste.
  2. Score fish and rub paste all over. Marinate 20–30 minutes.
  3. Grill or broil until flaky. Serve with fresh herbs and lime.
