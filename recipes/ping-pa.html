Ping Pa (Lao Grilled Fish)

Simply marinated fish grilled and served with jeow som (sour dipping sauce).

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 small whole fish or 2 fillets (tilapia/trout)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 lime, sliced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)

Steps

  1. Score fish and rub with garlic, ginger, and fish sauce; stuff lemongrass and lime inside.
  2. Grill over medium heat (or bake 400°F/205°C) until flaky, ~10–15 min for fillets.
  3. Serve with steamed rice and vegetables; optional jeow som dip.

Health Tips

Brush with minimal oil; use low‑sodium fish sauce or dilute with water.
