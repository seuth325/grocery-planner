Steamed Fish with Ginger & Scallion

  • 2 white-fish fillets
  • 2 in ginger, julienned; 2 scallions, shredded
  • 1 tbsp low-sodium soy + 1 tbsp water + 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  1. Steam fish 6–8 min until flaky.
  2. Warm soy mixture; pour over fish, top with ginger & scallion.
