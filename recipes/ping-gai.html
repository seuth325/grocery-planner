Ping Gai (Lao Grilled Chicken)

Garlic‑lemongrass marinated chicken grilled to juicy perfection.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1.5 lb chicken thighs
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (low sodium)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with lemongrass, garlic, lime, and sauces 30–60 min.
  2. Grill over medium heat until 165°F/74°C. Rest and slice.

Health Tips

Dilute sauces with water to reduce sodium; add more lime for brightness.
