Ping Gai (Lao Grilled Chicken)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb chicken thighs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, finely minced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with garlic, lemongrass, fish sauce, lime, honey, sesame oil 30–60 minutes.
  2. Grill or roast at 425°F/220°C until cooked through and charred at edges.
  3. Rest and slice. Serve with fresh herbs.
