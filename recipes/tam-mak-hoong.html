Tam Mak Hoong (Lao Papaya Salad)

Crunchy green papaya with lime, chilies, and a touch of funk.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 cups shredded green papaya
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 long beans, chopped
  • 1–2 Thai chilies, to taste
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp palm sugar or honey
  • 1–2 tsp fish sauce (low sodium)

Steps

  1. Pound chilies and sugar, then add long beans and tomatoes; bruise lightly.
  2. Toss with papaya, lime juice, and fish sauce; taste balance (sour/salty/sweet).

Health Tips

Go lighter on fish sauce; boost lime for brightness. Add toasted peanuts for protein.
