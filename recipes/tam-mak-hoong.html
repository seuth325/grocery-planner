Tam Mak Hoong (Lao Papaya Salad)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded green papaya (or cucumber + carrot matchsticks)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1–2 Thai chilies
  • 1–2 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1–2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp palm sugar or honey
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional)

Steps

  1. Pound chilies and garlic in a mortar (or mince).
  2. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, lime juice; adjust to taste sweet-sour-salty-hot.
  3. Toss with shredded papaya (or subs) and tomatoes. Serve immediately.
