Thai Green Curry with Shrimp

  • 8 oz shrimp, 1 cup light coconut milk
  • Green curry paste (1–2 tbsp), eggplant or bell pepper, snap peas
  • Fish sauce (1 tsp), basil, lime
  1. Simmer paste in a splash of coconut milk, add veg, then shrimp.
  2. Finish with fish sauce, basil, and lime.
