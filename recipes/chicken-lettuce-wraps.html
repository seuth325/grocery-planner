Chicken Lettuce Wraps

  • 10 oz lean ground chicken
  • Mushrooms, diced; garlic; water chestnuts (optional)
  • 2 tbsp low-sodium soy; 1 tsp rice vinegar; 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  1. Sauté chicken with mushrooms & garlic.
  2. Stir in soy, vinegar, sesame oil. Spoon into lettuce cups.
