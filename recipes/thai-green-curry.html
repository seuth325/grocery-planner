Thai Green Curry

Light coconut curry with tender veg and your protein of choice.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 tbsp green curry paste
  • 1 can light coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetables (bok choy, peppers)
  • 8 oz protein (tofu/chicken)
  • 1 tsp fish or soy sauce (low sodium)
  • Basil & lime to finish

Steps

  1. Bloom curry paste; add coconut milk and simmer.
  2. Add veg and protein; cook until tender. Season lightly and finish with basil and lime.

Health Tips

Use light coconut milk and go easy on the sauce; fresh herbs do the heavy lifting.
