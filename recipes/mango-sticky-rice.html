Mango Sticky Rice

A light, portion‑controlled take on a classic dessert.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 cup cooked sticky rice
  • 1 ripe mango
  • 1/3 cup light coconut milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Honey to taste

Steps

  1. Warm coconut milk with a pinch of salt; sweeten lightly with honey.
  2. Spoon over warm sticky rice and top with mango.

Health Tips

Use light coconut milk and modest portions.
