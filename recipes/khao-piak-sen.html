Khao Piak Sen (Lao Noodle Soup)

Silky hand‑pulled rice noodles in a gentle chicken broth.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 cups low‑sodium chicken broth
  • 1 in ginger, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 6 oz rice noodles (or hand‑pulled)
  • Shredded chicken (optional)
  • Green onions & cilantro

Steps

  1. Simmer broth with ginger and garlic for 10–15 min; strain if desired.
  2. Cook noodles separately; combine in bowls with broth and chicken.
  3. Top with herbs and a squeeze of lime.

Health Tips

Use low‑sodium broth; finish with fresh herbs for depth.
