Khao Piak Sen (Lao Chicken Noodle Soup)

Ingredients

  • 1 qt low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 chicken breast, poached & shredded
  • Fresh rice noodles (or rice noodles, soaked)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, sliced
  • Green onions & cilantro to finish
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Simmer broth with garlic and ginger 10 minutes; season lightly.
  2. Add noodles and cook until tender.
  3. Add shredded chicken. Serve with herbs and lime.
