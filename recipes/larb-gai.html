Larb Gai (Thai Minced Chicken Salad)

Ingredients (serves 2)

  • 10 oz ground chicken
  • 2 limes (juice)
  • 2 tbsp toasted rice powder
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (or 1 tbsp low-sodium fish sauce + 1 tbsp lime)
  • 1 tsp palm sugar or honey
  • 1 shallot, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 bunch mint, chopped; optional cilantro
  • Thai chilies, to taste

Method

  1. Dry-sauté chicken until just cooked. Cool 2 minutes.
  2. Toss with lime juice, fish sauce, sugar, rice powder, shallot, herbs, and chilies.
  3. Serve with lettuce cups or cucumber slices.
