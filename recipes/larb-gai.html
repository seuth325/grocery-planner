Larb Gai (Lao/Thai Minced Chicken Salad)

Bright, herby salad with lime, mint, and toasted rice powder.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 2 limes (juice)
  • 1 small bunch mint, chopped
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1–2 Thai chilies, minced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp palm sugar or honey
  • 1–2 tbsp toasted rice powder
  • Lettuce leaves for serving

Steps

  1. Brown ground chicken in a dry skillet until cooked; cool slightly.
  2. Stir in lime juice, fish sauce, palm sugar, chilies, shallots, and green onions.
  3. Fold in mint and toasted rice powder to bind. Taste and adjust acidity/salt.
  4. Serve in lettuce cups with extra herbs.

Health Tips

Use low‑sodium fish sauce; add salt at the end. Toasted rice powder thickens without oil.
