Light Chinese Vegetable Soup

Clear, soothing soup with mixed veg.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 4 cups low‑sodium veg broth
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Ginger coins
  • White pepper

Steps

  1. Simmer broth with ginger; add vegetables until tender.
  2. Season with white pepper.

Health Tips

Use low‑sodium broth; herbs/pepper add flavor without salt.
